Our Team

CART Jain

CA R T Jain is the senior most and founder member of the Firm. He qualified as a chartered accountant in the year 1982 and is into core practice since then. He has vast experience of over three decades in practice. He specializes in the field of Income Tax, Assessment and Appeals. CA R T Jain has accepted sainthood in the year 2020 and since then he is not professionally associated with the Firm.

CA Bankim Jain

CA Bankim Jain qualified in the year 2011. He specializes in the area of business advisory services, capital market services and fund raising.

CA Sweta Jain

CA Sweta Jain qualified in the year 2013. She completed her articleship from Deloitte Haskins & Sells. She specializes in Goods and Service Tax.

CA Bharat Jain

CA Bharat Jain is associated with the Firm since 2014. Migrating from a small village in Rajasthan to pursue a career in Chartered Accountancy in the City of Dreams, he joined as an article assistant and due to his ambitious vision and skill set he rose through the ranks to become a partner in the Firm. He specializes in the area of debt syndication, Audit & Assurance and compliance.

Suryakant Raut

Suryakant Raut is associated with the Firm since 2004. He has been directly trained under our chief mentor CA R T Jain and had been actively assisting him in Direct Tax Representation. He specializes in the area of Direct Tax.

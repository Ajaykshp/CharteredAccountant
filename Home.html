ABOUT

Our firm is a culmination of three decades of practice in a wide array of Business Advisory, Audit & Assurance, Direct Taxes, Indirect Taxes and compliance with a team of chartered accountants and professionals fostered under the wisdom of CA R T Jain. Our focus shall always be the best decision tailor made to the requirements and needs of our clients. We strive no less than excellence in our service to our clientele.

We have a culture of professionalism reflecting the ethos of chartered accountancy, and have an environment to nurture future chartered accountants as article clerks.Our firm has been peer reviewed and issued peer review certificate by the ICAI.

Services Offered

DIRECT TAX COMPLIANCES

Behind success comes Income Tax and everyone must pay their fair dues to the government. Correct timely compliance and proper tax planning saves time, money and reflects an image of an honest individual. At RT Jain and Co LLP, we ensure all income tax compliances from registrations to refunds, our services include

  • Direct Tax Dispute Representation.
  • Yearly tax health check up.
  • Direct Tax Advisory for tax planning.
  • Filing of Return of Income Tax, Withholding Tax and Various Forms under Income Tax statute.
  • Obtaining Lower Deduction Certificate.

INDIRECT TAX COMPLIANCES

Indirect Taxation is the majority of the nation’s revenue and many businesses have issues in it’s compliance. We provide a holistic array of services to help businesses comply with the indirect taxation including

  • Indirect Tax Registration.
  • Filing of various returns under Indirect Tax.
  • Indirect Tax Dispute Representation.
  • Assistance in obtaining refund under Indirect Tax.

AUDIT ASSURANCE AND CERTIFICATION SERVICES

Rising from a firm to a corporate company is a radical shift in compliance, in the everchanging corporate world compliance takes up too much time to do core business, to ensure timely compliance with the Companies Act, SEBI (LODR) and MCA, we provide services including -

  • Statutory Audit.
  • Tax Audit.
  • GST Audit.
  • Bank Branch Audit.
  • Insurance Audit.
  • Limited Review of Results.
  • Transfer Pricing Audit.
  • Financial Due Diligence.
  • Issuance of Various Statutory Certificates.

BUSINESS / TRANSACTION ADVISORY

An IPO / PE/ M&A is the start of a big phase of a company, this phase is a huge influx of money and a total business overhaul. To assist in this crucial process we provide services including-

  • PE deal advisory.
  • Virtual CFO Services.
  • Handholding services for startups since Incorporation.
  • M&A deal advisory.
  • Employee Compensation advisory.
  • Business Structuring Advisory.
  • Due Diligence.
  • Valuation Services.
  • Investment Advisory Services.

FUND RAISING SERVICES

The role of Chartered Accountants are not limited to the areas of audit and taxation, the world of finance is colossal and at every point there is an area of facilitation, RT Jain and Co LLP provides financial services including–

  • Debt Syndication.
  • Venture Capital Fund Raising.
  • Private Equity Fund Raising.
  • Fund Raising through IPO.
